What SMRITI is

Not another RAG chatbot. A tri-modal operations brain.

One question fans out across text, a knowledge graph, and the drawings themselves — fused, reranked, and answered on the P&ID with citations.

01 · Fusion

Text + graph + visual, in one answer

"Why does P-101 trip?" needs drawing connectivity + work-order history + a visually-retrieved OEM page. A text-only RAG can't produce it.

02 · Vision

OCR-free visual retrieval

Every page indexed as an image (colSmol-256M, patch-level MaxSim). Scanned, stamped, even a 1958 legacy drawing — retrieved on visual signal alone.

03 · Reasoning

The graph connects what no human does

RCA walks the fleet to surface the monsoon seal pattern across P-101 / P-103 / P-107 — and matches tomorrow's permit to a 2019 near-miss.

04 · Trust

Every sentence cites; "insufficient evidence" is allowed

Citations click through to the exact page. Faithfulness is measured, not claimed.
